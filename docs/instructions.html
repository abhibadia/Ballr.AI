Instructions

1. Press the Upload button to select a video file in .mp4 format from your device. Your video should capture the basketball play you want to analyze.

2. Once your video is uploaded, click the Analyze button. Our system will process the footage and break down the plays to give you optimized offensive and defensive strategies.

3. When the analysis is complete, you’ll receive detailed recommendations including what play is being made, what formation, and the most optimized offensive and defensive strategy against a play to improve your game instantly. It’s quick and easy to get the insights you need with Ballr AI!
