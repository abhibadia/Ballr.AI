About Us

We’re Abhi and Surya, the creators of Ballr AI. Our mission is to help basketball players outside of the college and professional levels access the kind of advanced analysis that’s usually reserved for elite athletes. We built Ballr AI to empower players like us — high school athletes and rec league ballers — with powerful tools to elevate their game. We believe that with the right insights, anyone can sharpen their skills and understanding of basketball. Ballr AI is here to make that possible for everyone, no matter where you play. Let’s raise the bar together!
